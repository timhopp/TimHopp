A Bit About Me

I've never been one to back away from a challenge. From working for a week in -15 degree weather in Montana to traversing canyons on nothing more than a 1 inch wide rope, I've always been attracted to things that seem impossible at first.

While coding certainly isn't impossible, it has its fair share of challenges. Whether it's fixing a tricky bug, dealing with a difficult API, or learning to implement a new feature, success in coding often comes only with perseverance. This neccessity for perseverance, combined with problem solving and creative, is what makes me passionate about coding. There is no better feeling than working through a difficult problem and finally finding a solution.
